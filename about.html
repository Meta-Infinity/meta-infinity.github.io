About Our Team

Meta^Infinity (Team 18221) is a dedicated group of high school students passionate about robotics and engineering. Founded in 2020, we compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge, pushing the boundaries of innovation and technology.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower the next generation of innovators through robotics while promoting STEM education in our community.

Core Values

  • Innovation and Creative Problem Solving
  • Teamwork and Collaboration
  • Gracious Professionalism
  • Community Engagement
